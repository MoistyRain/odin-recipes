Mediterranean Chicken Sheet Pan Dinner

Description

"Bold flavors and colors combine on one sheet pan for a simple yet impressive dinner. Bonus is the minimal cleanup!" - Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin oil
  • lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paparika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 chicken thighs with skin
  • 1 small red onion, sliced into petals
  • 8 mini bell peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 8 pitted kalamata olives
