Mexican Street Tacos

Description

"Easy, quick, authentic, carne asada street tacos you can now make right at home!" - damndelicious.com

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil, divided
  • 3 cloves
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 1⁄2 pounds skirt steak
  • 3/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 lime

Directions

  1. Combine soy sauce, lime juice, 1 tbsp canola oil, garlic chili powder, cumin, and oregano in a medium bowl
  2. In a gallon Ziploc bag or large bowl, combine soy sauce mixture and steak; marinate for at least 1 hour up to 4 hours, turning the bag occasionally.
  3. Heat remaining 1 tbsp of canola oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add steak and marinade, and cook, stirring often, until steak has browned and marinade has reduced, about 5-6 minutes, or until desired doneness.
  4. Serve steak in tortillas, topped with onion, cilantro and lime
